FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is where you turn your crazy ideas into real stuff. Hundreds of hackers gather around and build something they’re passionate about. You take care of hacking it out and we’ll take care of you.

Who can come?

Royal Hacks is open to any enrolled college student (undergrad or grad) from anywhere in the world.

What if I don’t know how to code?

You can have 4 to a team.

What if I don’t have a team or idea?

Chill! We'll have some fun team-forming activities to help you find your team members. Also, many hackers come up with great ideas while they're at Royal Hacks.

What does it cost?

Royal Hacks is free for all accepted hackers. We provide meals, caffeine, swag, workspace, and WiFi for the weekend.

Do you offer travel reimbursments?

At this time, no.
